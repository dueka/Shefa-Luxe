Our story

Shefa Luxe was born out of a desire for innovation in Fashion. Shefa Luxe was launched in 2018, since it's inception and has quickly racked up a following for it's unique blend of luxury, style and comfort.

Shefa Luxe is defined by the use of Exquisite materials and expertise craftmanship with all garments hand made. The designs of Shefa Luxe are inspired by her desire for luxury and worldwide travels. Shefa Luxe has collaborated to produce unique collections.

Shefa Luxe's interests greatly influence the designs and the unique range of Fashion styling, combining personal style with an academic design background.

Shefa Luxe's Spring Collection has been carried by exclusive retailers including Belangelique and many more. In addition to the lengthy list of Luxury Retail Partners, Shefa also counts Chioma goodhair, Juliet Ibrahim, Gigi Jasmine, Kiki Osinbanjo, Nafama Evans.
